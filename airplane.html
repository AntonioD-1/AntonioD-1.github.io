Airplane

Near the end of the Industrial Revolution, Wilbur and Orville Wright invented the first airplane. Previously, they had studied kites and birds to find out how flight worked. By 1903, the first flight was made near Kitty Hawk, North Carolina and lasted almost a minute. The Wright brothers first sold an airplane in 1909.
