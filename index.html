What is the Industrial Revolution?

The Industrial Revolution lasted from 1760 to the 1830s. It began in Great Britian. Many new building and inventing techniques were discovered during this time. The steam engine, photograph, cement, airplane, and other inventions we still use today are a direct product of the Industrial Revolution.
