The Cotton Gin

Eli Whitney invented the cotton gin in 1793. The machine separates cotton fibers from seeds, cleaning it out and allowing it to be used in clothing. This lead to a large cotton increase in the US. However, it also increased slavery in the South, as cotton planters could now earn more profit.
